BANKING AND INSURANCE

MBA

MBA in Banking and Insurance is post graduate Banking course.

The course covers concepts and training in subjects such as International Banking and Insurance, Project and Infrastructure, Investment Banking etc.


COURSE INFORMATION

DURATION

2 Years

ELIGIBILITY

Candidates who have completed Bachelor’s degree in any stream can pursue MBA in Banking and Insurance.
