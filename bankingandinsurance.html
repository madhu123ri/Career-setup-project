CAREER SETUP

BANKING AND INSURANCE

BCom

It is a 3-year bachelor’s degree course, which is generally divided into 6 semesters. The main subjects in this course are economics, management of financial services, banking & insurance, financial accounting, business law, financial management, customer relationship management, Information Technology in Banking, International banking & finance etc.


COURSE INFORMATION


DURATION

3 Years

ELIGIBILITY

Student must have passed the Higher Secondary School Certificate ( 12th ) examination.

PATHWAY

12th Passed in any stream --> B.Com. in Banking and Insurance
