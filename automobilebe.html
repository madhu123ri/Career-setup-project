CAREER SETUP

AUTOMOBILE ENGINEERING

BE

It is a 4-year bachelor’s degree course. This course includes subjects like Production Technology, Environmental Science and Engineering, Applied Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics and Machinery, Mechanics of Machines, Fuels and Lubricants, Automotive Diesel engines, etc.


COURSE INFORMATION


DURATION

4 Years

ELIGIBILITY

Passed 12th examination with subjects Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in the course.

PATHWAY

Class X/12th with Physics, Chemistry, Maths --> Diploma in Automobile Engineering -->B.E./ B.Tech (Automobile/ Mechanical - Admission into 2nd year Engg) --> M.E. or M.tech in Automobile Engg OR 12th with Physics, Chemistry, Maths --> B.E in Automobile/ Mechanical Engineering --> M.E. Automobile Engineering 12th with Physics, Chemistry, Maths --> B.Sc. In Physics/ Chemistry/ Maths (with Physics as a subject) --> B. Tech in Automobile Engineering

Job opputunities after this course:



It is a specialized branch of Mechanical Engineering. Automobile Engineers design and develop new vehicles and modes of transport. They design them keeping in mind factors like performance, economy, comfort, maintenance, environmental impact and looks. They also lend their expertise to upgrade existing models and work out which parts of the vehicle need to be replaced with new and improved parts.

1) Manufacturing plants such as Maruti Udyog Ltd., Telco, Fiat, etc

2) After considerable experience one can open one’s own service shop of repairing old vehicles and modifying new ones as per the customers' wishes. Eg: Dilip Chhabria Designs

3) One can also work as surveyors for insurance companies provided you have a licence from the central government

4) One can also freelance as a writer for technical journals, car magazines and websites
