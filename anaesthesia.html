It is a 2-years course and students who have passed MBBS are eligible for this course. Anaesthesiology focuses on utilization of drugs that minimize pain by causing insensibility. Under this degree, students learn about areas such as ambulatory anesthesia, cardiothoracic anesthesiology, pediatric anesthesiology, neuroanesthesia, obstetrical anesthesiology and pain management. Anesthesia controls pain during surgery or other medical procedures. It includes using medicines, and sometimes closes monitoring, to keep you comfortable. Job opputunities after this course:



An anesthesiologist is a physician who is specialized in the practice of anesthesiology, a branch of medicine which involves the use of drugs in surgical processes to make a surgery as painless as possible. Often people confuse an Anesthesiologist with an Anesthetist. The main difference being, an anesthetist maybe a nurse or technician trained to administer anesthetics whereas an anesthesiologist has a doctorate.
