Architects design the overall aesthetic & look of building and other structures, but the design of a building involves far more than its appearance. Building must also be functional, safe, & economical and must suit the needs of people who use them. Architects consider all these factors when they design these building and other structures.

Their duties require specific skills – designing, engineering, managing, supervising, & communication with clients and builders.
