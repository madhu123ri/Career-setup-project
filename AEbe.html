CAREER SETUP

AERONAUTICAL ENGINEERING

BE

It is a 4-year bachelor's degree course. This degree course mainly focuses on the fundamentals of propulsion, electronics, and automatic control guidance, theory of aerodynamics, structural analysis, material science, and fluid dynamics.


COURSE INFORMATION


DURATION

4 Years

ELIGIBILITY

Passed 12th examination with subjects Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in the course.

PATHWAY

12th with Physics, Chemistry, Maths --> B. Tech in Aerospace Engineering OR B.E or B. Tech in Mechanical/Electrical/Electronics Engineering --> M.E/ M. Tech in Aeronautical Engineering

ENTRANCE EXAMS

JEE
state wise engg entrance exams

Job opputunities after this course:



Aerospace Engineers are generally appointed as Consultants, Thermal Design Engineers, Mechanical Design Engineers, Aerospace Technologist, Aircraft Production Manager, Assistant Technical Officers, Aerospace Design Checker and Graduate Engineer Trainees.
