CAREER SETUP

ANIMATION

BSc

This course in Animation is ideal for people seeking their future in Graphic Design, Web Design, Animation and Digital Artist. Students may specialize in one particular module but they will have knowledge in all other aspects of Animation. Thus, one does not become redundant with a change in the work profile due to one's versatility.
